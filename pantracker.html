01/ The Genesis

The Friction

The project originated from a recurring personal frustration: "Inventory Amnesia." Standing in a grocery aisle, unable to recall if a specific ingredient was already stocked at home, led to either redundant purchases or mis-recipe readiness of skipping essentials.

Existing solutions felt bloated or required manual auditing that felt more like a chore than a tool. I envisioned a digital twin with assistive intelligence that lived where the friction did.

The Concept

A lightweight, persistent digital mirror of your pantry — designed with a zero-friction input model and a proactive notification engine. Not another to-do list, but an intelligent inventory companion.

05/ Engineering Highlights
Under the Hood

The system architecture follows a clean MVVM pattern with strict SwiftUI principles, ensuring the codebase is as maintainable as it is performant.

Built with Core Data for persistence, Combine for reactive data flow, and SwiftUI for a declarative UI layer that stays in sync with the underlying model at all times.
